# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, respectful, and inclusive community.

- Be kind and constructive. Assume good intent.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Keep discussions technical and on-topic.
- Respect differing opinions and experiences.
- Follow project maintainersâ€™ guidance.

Enforcement
- Report abusive or unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via email or GitHub.
- Maintainers may take action they deem appropriate (warnings, issue/PR moderation, or bans).

Scope
- Applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues/PRs/discussions, community chats, and any public representation of the project.

This policy is adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
